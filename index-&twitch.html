Embed YouTube & Twitch Player

Enter a YouTube or Twitch URL or ID below, then select the platform

How to use:

Enter a YouTube video ID, YouTube URL, Twitch channel name, Twitch clip URL, or Twitch VOD URL, then select the platform (YouTube/Twitch).

Add the video/channel ID at the end of the URL after the question mark:

siteUrl/?MrJXtHG1D0E (for YouTube)
siteUrl/?monstercat (for Twitch channel)

You can also copy the current URL after loading a video and share it - the content will automatically load when the link is opened.


javascript:(function(){const v=new URL(location.href).searchParams.get('v');if(!v)return;window.location.href=`https://mbugr.github.io/Example_Github_Page/?${v}`})();


Supported Twitch URLs:

  • Channel: https://www.twitch.tv/channelName
  • Clip: https://clips.twitch.tv/clipName or https://www.twitch.tv/.../clip/clipName
  • VOD: https://www.twitch.tv/videos/videoId
